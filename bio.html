Grant Winter

Biography

Grant Winter was born in Liverpool, England. At the age of 3 his family moved to Canada to Engage in the fur trade. It was here that he discoved he could speak with bears in their native tongue.

Finding it lonely during the winter due to his good friends hibernating he relocated back to the UK in 2004, where he stayed for 9 years. Tiring of the dimal weather on offer in the United Kingdom, he then decided to move back to Canada, as he just couldn't get enough of the cold, and the snow.

His current companions include a lovley wife who is a veterinarian and his trusty 3 legged cat, Julius. Julius lost his right arm in a Saskachewan gun fight.
